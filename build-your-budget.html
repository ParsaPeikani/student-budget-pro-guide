💡

Key Takeaways

  • ✓ A budget is a plan, not a punishment. It tells your money where to go instead of wondering where it went.
  • ✓ Review and adjust every week. No budget is perfect on the first try — and that is okay.
  • ✓ Your budget should always be: Income - Needs - Wants - Savings = zero or positive. If negative, cut wants first.
  • ✓ Print or save your budget and keep it visible. Out of sight means out of mind.
